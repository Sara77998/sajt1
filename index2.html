Adventures

Winter wonderland

When winter is upon us, the temptation is to retreat to our homes for cosy nights in. But all around the world, winter is the time when nature really shines. For some travelers, a winter wonderland of snow is what this season is all about. For others, the escape from sub-zero temperatures is a must. Yhese trips will have just what you are looking for.

Our propositions for winter:

  • Rovaniemi, Finland
  • Lake Bled, Slovenia
  • Hallstatt, Austria

Summer

The power of wanderlust is real. Catch a glimpse of these gorgeous destinations across the globe, and you'll start packing your bags before you now it. For many, the desire to travel peaks when summer rolls around and with so many places experiencing warmer weather and longer days, it's hard to decide which vacation is right for you.

Great destinations for summer:

  • Ubud, Bali
  • Havana, Cuba
  • Santorini, Greece

Cities

We seek beauty in many forms—through art and architecture; water views and mountain vistas; history and culture—and some places make that search extremely easy. These are the cities that make us fall in love at first sight with their futuristic skylines, tree-lined canals, and colorful colonial buildings (among many other things).

Visit beautiful cities:

  • New York, USA
  • Tokyo, Japan
  • London, Britain
